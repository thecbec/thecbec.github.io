<p>Sponsored by the Chinese Scholarship Council and hosted by Huazhong Agricultural University, the 2024 International Cross-Border E-commerce Summer School Program will be held in Yili City, Xinjiang Autonomous Region, China, from August 5th to August 22nd.</p>



<p><strong>1. Content of Training</strong></p>



<p>The program will be conducted in the real business context of cross-border e-commerce. Experienced entrepreneurs in the field will share their knowledge and provide hands-on training. The course will cover basic e-commerce theory and intensive practice based on real business scenarios.</p>



<p>Trainees will have the opportunity to visit the Khorgos border between China and Kazakhstan.</p>



<p>Excellent trainees may find potential business partners in China.</p>

<p><strong>2. Eligibility</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Undergraduate students, postgraduate students, teachers, researchers, entreprenurs from Afghanistan, Pakistan. Egypt. Uganda, Bangladesh. Sri Lanka. Kenya. Uzbekistan. Russia, Mozambique, Cote D'ivoire, Ethiopia.</li>



<li>Have an interest in cross border e-commerce and trade.</li>



<li>Good English communication skills.</li>



<li>International students already studying in China are <strong>NOT </strong>eligible to apply.</li>
</ul>
