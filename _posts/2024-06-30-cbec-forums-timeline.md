<div class="cbec-timeline">
<div class="cbec-event">
<h3>2018 - Wuhan, China</h3>
<p>The inaugural forum at Huazhong Agricultural University marked the beginning of our journey. Key highlights included:</p>
<ul>
<li>8 academic presentations analyzing e-commerce landscapes across countries</li>
<li>Visits to innovative companies like Loulanmiyu</li>
<li>A field trip to Alibaba Group headquarters in Hangzhou</li>
<li>Tour of Yiwu International Trade City</li>
</ul>
<p>This forum laid the foundation for future collaborations and sparked enthusiasm for cross-border e-commerce.</p>
</div>
<div class="cbec-event">
<h3>2019 - Faisalabad, Pakistan</h3>
<p>Our second forum demonstrated significant evolution:</p>
<ul>
<li>Extensive surveys conducted across Pakistan, including remote Gilgit-Baltistan region</li>
<li>Exploration of successful e-commerce models, particularly China's Taobao</li>
<li>Launch of the Sino-Pak CBEC Association</li>
<li>High-quality academic sessions with experts from partnering universities</li>
</ul>
<p>This forum deepened our understanding of local contexts and strengthened international partnerships.</p>
</div>
<div class="cbec-event">
<h3>2020 - Virtual Event</h3>
<p>Adapting to the challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic, we hosted our first online forum:</p>
<ul>
<li>4-hour agenda connecting over 100 participants across 7 interactive sessions</li>
<li>Focus on CBEC's potential to foster international cooperation and development</li>
<li>Sessions customized for Bangladesh, involving local universities</li>
<li>Participants from China, Bangladesh, Pakistan, Mongolia, Kenya, and Egypt</li>
</ul>
<p>This forum demonstrated our resilience and commitment to continued collaboration despite global challenges.</p>
</div>
<div class="cbec-event">
<h3>2021 - Jingzhou, China</h3>
<p>Returning to an in-person format, this forum focused on pressing global issues:</p>
<ul>
<li>Discussions centered on food security challenges and COVID-19 impacts</li>
<li>Academic session on supply chain research</li>
<li>Business session featuring case studies from e-commerce enterprises</li>
<li>Establishment of the Belt and Road Center for Rice Technology Extension and Training (BRC-Rice)</li>
</ul>
<p>This forum marked a significant step in addressing real-world challenges through CBEC.</p>
</div>
<div class="cbec-event">
<h3>2022 - Wuhan, China</h3>
<p>Our fifth forum expanded collaborations and deepened our research focus:</p>
<ul>
<li>Joint organization with Zhongnan University of Economics and Law</li>
<li>Focus on food security, poverty, and e-commerce in Belt and Road countries</li>
<li>Speakers representing China, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Kenya, Mongolia, and more</li>
<li>Paper presentations and panel discussions on field survey findings and policy insights</li>
</ul>
<p>This forum showcased our evolution from practical learning to research leadership.</p>
</div>
<div class="cbec-event">
<h3>2023 - Kashgar, China &amp; Gilgit, Pakistan</h3>
<p>Our sixth forum expanded across borders:</p>
<ul>
<li>Joint organization by Huazhong Agricultural University and Karakoram International University</li>
<li>Focus on cross-border e-commerce development and agricultural digital services</li>
<li>Emphasis on solving trust issues in cross-border e-commerce</li>
<li>Utilization of overseas student resources to improve cross-border efficiency</li>
</ul>
<p>This forum strengthened the China-Pakistan partnership in CBEC development.</p>
</div>
<div class="cbec-event">
<h3>2024 - Dhaka &amp; Mymensingh, Bangladesh</h3>
<p>Our seventh forum focused on microfinance and on the future of CBEC:</p>
<ul>
<li>Organized by University of Liberal Arts Bangladesh (ULAB) and Huazhong Agricultural University</li>
<li>Focus on sustainable micro-finance initiatives</li>
<li>Launch of the DD Fund</li>
<li>Promotion of e-commerce cooperation between China and Bangladesh</li>
</ul>
<p>This forum aimed to further expand our reach and impact in South Asia.</p>
</div>
<div class="cbec-event">
<h3>2025 - Kunming, China</h3>
<p>The eighth forum advanced digital agriculture and cross-border collaboration:</p>
<ul>
<li>Hosted by Huazhong Agricultural University and Yunnan Agricultural University</li>
<li>Theme: "Digital Empowerment and Global Connectivity: Cross-Border E-Commerce and High-Quality Agricultural Development"</li>
<li>Keynotes and panels on value creation, technology adoption, and regional cooperation</li>
<li>Parallel sessions on agricultural digital transformation, supply chain innovation, and talent development</li>
</ul>
<p>This forum reinforced international dialogue on cross-border e-commerce and high-quality agricultural development.</p>
</div>
