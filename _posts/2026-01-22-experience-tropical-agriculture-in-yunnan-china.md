<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Activities</h2>

<div class="wp-block-group" style="border-width:1px;border-radius:10px;margin-top:10px;margin-bottom:14px;padding-top:14px;padding-right:18px;padding-bottom:14px;padding-left:18px">
  <p><strong>Tea-Forest Mutualism Tour</strong> <span style="opacity:.75">(4 days)</span><br />Explore Jingmaishan, Puer, and join interaction sessions with local students.</p>
</div>

<div class="wp-block-group" style="border-width:1px;border-radius:10px;margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:14px;padding-top:14px;padding-right:18px;padding-bottom:14px;padding-left:18px">
  <p><strong>Architecture &amp; Cultural Heritage Tour</strong> <span style="opacity:.75">(6 days)</span><br />Jianshui County architecture tour; Honghe (Yuanyang) Hani rice terraces; Hani embroidery experience in Honghe Hani Autonomous Prefecture.</p>
</div>

<div class="wp-block-group" style="border-width:1px;border-radius:10px;margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:22px;padding-top:14px;padding-right:18px;padding-bottom:14px;padding-left:18px">
  <p><strong>Dehong Cultural Immersion</strong> <span style="opacity:.75">(5 days)</span><br />Learn about Sour Tea traditions, Achang Brocade weaving; project discussion and wrap-up at Dehong Vocational School.</p>
</div>
