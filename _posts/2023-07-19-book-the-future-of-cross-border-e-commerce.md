<p>In 2019, we published an excellent book titled "The Future of Cross Border E-Commerce" based on the proceedings of the International Cross Border E-Commerce (CBEC) Forum we held in 2018.</p>



<p>The book provides a comprehensive look at the rapid expansion of e-commerce across international borders. It includes chapters written by professors and researchers from institutions in China, India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Mongolia, Kenya, Egypt, Thailand and Barbados covering a diverse range of CBEC topics.</p>



<p>Some key insights the book provides include:</p>



<ul>
<li>An in-depth examination of the factors driving China's success as the world leader in e-commerce.</li>



<li>An overview of India's new national e-commerce policy and task force aimed at fueling growth in the sector.</li>



<li>Analysis of e-commerce development in emerging markets like Pakistan, Bangladesh, Mongolia and Kenya.</li>



<li>A case study of Alibaba's "Taobao villages" model bringing e-commerce to rural areas of China and its potential for replication.</li>



<li>Exploration of how blockchain technology could transform intellectual property protection in cross border e-commerce.</li>



<li>Assessments of consumer perspectives and experiences with e-commerce in multiple countries.</li>
</ul>



<p>In total, the book represents a comprehensive international perspective on the current CBEC landscape and future opportunities. We found the insights highly valuable for anyone looking to better understand the growth of global e-commerce.</p>
